How to cook fried rice in a proper way

Fried rice is a very simple dish, yet could be creative and tasteful. Today we teach how to make your simple fried rice extra fancy!

    Cut onions, ham and lettuce to a size of dice. York the egg in the meantime.

    Put wok (or large size pan) on heat, nice and hot. Then put some oil in.

    Start cooking ham first, 30 seconds later, add onion and green peas. Shake the wok frequently

    Add chopped garic, lettuce with salt and pepper.

    Right after, add rice in weaker heat.

    Add egg at the end. Cook for 30 more seconds
